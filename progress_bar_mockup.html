The bar is 10px tall with 5px rounded ends (pill shape). It updates every 100 ms via QTimer in the main window's _refresh_ui() method. The fill width = (cycle_count / target_cycles) × bar_width px. The percentage label to the right ("72 %") is a QLabel updated at the same interval.
